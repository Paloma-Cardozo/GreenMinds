The Problem

Critical Failure
Constant difficulty keeping plants alive at home

Our Solution

Product
Centralized platform with reliable botanical information
Action
Guided care with precise watering, light, and soil routines
Impact
Dramatic reduction of plant waste and successful growers

Security Architecture

JWT Flow

  • Registration: Passwords hashed with bcrypt (10 rounds)
  • Authentication: Email verification + rate limiting
  • Token Generation: JSON Web Tokens for sessions
  • Protected Routes: Bearer token validation for private data
