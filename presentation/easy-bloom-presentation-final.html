The Problem

Critical Failure
Constant difficulty keeping plants alive at home
Data Deficiency
Inability to find and apply basic care rules
Sustainability Loss
Biological waste from discarding dead plants

Our Solution

Product
Centralized platform with reliable botanical information
Action
Guided care with precise watering, light, and soil routines
Impact
Dramatic reduction of plant waste and successful growers

Key Features

  • Botanical Database with 5000+ plants
  • Advanced Search by name and difficulty
  • Secure Authentication (JWT)
  • Private Collections (Favorites)
  • Care Guides with detailed requirements
