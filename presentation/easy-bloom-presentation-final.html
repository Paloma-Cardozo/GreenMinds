The Problem

You Dream

You dream of a beautiful, green home. Imagining plants everywhere, bright windows, and fresh air. It would make you so happy!

You Buy

One day, you find the perfect plant at the store. You bring it home, so excited and hopeful! Finally, your dream is becoming real.

You Search

But then you realize... how do you take care of it? Does it need lots of water? A sunny spot? How often? You search everywhere for answers.

It Dies

Days pass, and something goes wrong... The leaves turn brown and start to fall. Your beautiful plant is struggling and you don't know why.

The Waste

Sadly, this happens to millions of plants every year. People throw them away because they don't know how to care for them. So much beauty and life... lost.

The Real Story: During the pandemic, 68% of Americans turned to houseplants for comfort. Half of young adults (Gen Z) bought plants specifically to improve their mental health. Yet 7 in 10 Gen Zers lost at least one plant, and 33% abandoned the hobby entirely. The main reason? They didn't know how to care for them properly. (Source: Trees.com Survey, 2022)

Easy Bloom: Our Solution

We are four women who love plants and believe in making the world greener and more sustainable. We've been there, struggling with plant care, searching for answers, watching our green friends fade. So we created Easy Bloom App as a tool to help you succeed where we once failed.

The Product

A smart platform that learns your plant's needs and teaches you exactly what to do. Simple, beautiful, and always there when your plant needs help.

Our Action

We provide clear, kind guidance on watering, sunlight, and plant health. No confusion. No dead plants. Just you and your thriving plant friends.

Our Dream

Easy Bloom App becomes a training tool for plant care. A world where plants thrive, and people grow.

Our Data Source

Our third step was finding reliable plant information. Think of an API like a bridge between services. Netflix works on your phone, TV, and computer. Each one uses an API to talk to Netflix's servers. We needed the same thing: a trusted source with plant care knowledge that our app could connect to.

OpenPlantBook

We chose OpenPlantBook because none of us had gardening experience. We needed a reliable source with real plant data.

Database contains thousands of plant species with detailed care instructions

How We Store Information

Next, we designed how to safely store our information. We decided we needed three connected tables working together. Each one has a specific job, and they link perfectly to create the complete story of our app.

Users Table

We store each person who joins Easy Bloom. Their username, email, and protected password.

  • Unique ID number
  • Username and email
  • Secure password
  • Join date

Plants Table

We save information about each plant. The PlantBook ID, common name, and plant image. Care details come fresh from the API.

  • Plant ID from PlantBook
  • Common name (alias)
  • Plant image URL
  • Date added

Favorites Table

We connect users to plants they love. This table remembers which person saved which plant.

  • Connection ID
  • User ID
  • Plant ID
  • When they saved it

This design keeps user passwords safe, lets us find plants instantly, and remembers who loves what. All three tables work together as one complete system.

Fun fact: We bring five care details from PlantBook: sunlight, watering, soil, fertilization, and pruning. We don't store these here. They come fresh every time you request them, so you always have the most current information.

While We Built It

As we coded and designed, we thought constantly about hackers and security. We built multiple layers of protection so only you can access your information safely.

Check Everything

Before we save your information, we verify it is correct. Email must be valid format, password must be at least 8 characters long, and cannot contain spaces.

Secret Code

Your password is never stored as you typed it. We transform it into unreadable code. Even if someone breaks in, they cannot read your original password.

Temporary Pass

When you log in, we give you a pass. For 7 days you can enter the app without typing your password again. After 7 days, you must log in once more to stay secure.

Smart Limits

After 5 failed login attempts, the account locks for 15 minutes. This stops attackers from guessing passwords thousands of times.

No Doubles

We check that no two people have the same email or username. Each person gets only one account, so your identity stays unique and protected.

Plants

User Profile

Your Information

  • Update profile: Change your name or email
  • Change password: Update your password anytime
  • Delete account: Remove your account if you want

We Tested Everything

After writing all the code, we needed to check that everything worked correctly. We used a tool called Postman to test our API. Think of it like a quality inspector that clicks buttons and checks if the app responds the right way.

We tested 63 scenarios

Every signup, login, search, favorite, and error case. We made sure passwords were protected, tokens worked, and hackers couldn't break in.
